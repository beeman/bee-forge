Bee Forge

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

Bee Forge is a developer-focused app for Solana devnet experiments. It does not intentionally collect, sell, or share personal information.

App state, authentication state, and wallet-related preferences may be stored locally on your device. Bee Forge does not operate a hosted account service for this site.

Wallet providers, app stores, browsers, GitHub Pages, or Solana network services may process information under their own policies when you use their services.

To request removal of information you believe was provided to Bee Forge, contact the project maintainer through the Bee Forge GitHub repository.
