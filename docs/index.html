Bee Forge

Bee Forge is a small Solana devnet companion app for learning, testing, and experimenting with wallet flows.

The app includes local Sign in with Solana auth, Mobile Wallet Adapter support, wallet actions, and placeholder tools for upcoming devnet utilities.

Bee Forge is built as an Expo app with Bun, Expo Router, HeroUI Native, Uniwind, and Wallet UI.
